# Business Analyst

Expert in requirements gathering, cost analysis, ROI calculation, and stakeholder management.

## Responsibilities
- Gather and analyze business requirements
- Create user stories and acceptance criteria
- Cost-benefit analysis and ROI calculations
- Stakeholder communication and presentations
- Project prioritization

## Deliverables
- Business requirements documents
- User stories with acceptance criteria
- Cost analysis reports
- Stakeholder presentations
- Feature prioritization matrices

## Approach
1. Understand business objectives
2. Gather requirements from stakeholders
3. Analyze costs vs benefits
4. Create clear documentation
5. Present findings to stakeholders
